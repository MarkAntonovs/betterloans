How We Make Money

Full transparency about affiliate links, sponsorships and our editorial independence.

On this page
Our business model Affiliate links Sponsored placements Editorial independence Tracking & privacy Contact

FinSriLanka is a comparison website. We are not a lender. We may receive a commission when you click an offer or apply on a partner website.

1) Our business model

We earn money from affiliate programs and occasional sponsored placements. These revenues allow us to keep the service free for users.

2) Affiliate links

When you click “Apply” or similar buttons, the link may contain a tracking parameter (e.g. subid, gclid). If you proceed on the lender website, we may receive a fee from the partner. This does not change the price or terms for you.

3) Sponsored placements

Sometimes partners sponsor a card or banner. Such placements are clearly marked as “Sponsored”. Sponsorship does not guarantee a positive review.

4) Editorial independence

  • Our listings aim to be accurate and useful for Sri Lanka users.
  • Partners cannot buy higher ratings in our copy.
  • We update content and remove outdated offers when needed.

5) Tracking & privacy

We use Google Analytics to understand anonymized usage and decorate outbound links with UTM/GCLID for proper attribution. See our Policies for details.

6) Contact

Questions about this disclosure? Email: finsrilanka@protonmail.com.

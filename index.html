Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about online loans in Sri Lanka.

Do I get 0% interest on my first loan?

Some providers offer introductory 0% interest to first-time borrowers. Availability and limits vary by lender and eligibility.

What documents do I need?

Typically, your NIC and a Sri Lankan bank account. Some lenders may request additional information (e.g., phone number or proof of income).

Is FinSriLanka safe?

We don’t collect loan applications. We connect you with providers. Always review the lender’s security, privacy policy, and terms before applying.

What happens after I click “Apply”?

You’ll be redirected to the lender’s website to complete your application.

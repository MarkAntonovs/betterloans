Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about online loans in Sri Lanka.

Do I really get 0% interest on my first loan?

Yes! Many of our partner lenders offer 0% interest for first-time users. This applies to your first loan with each respective lender.

What documents do I need to apply?

You typically only need your NIC (National ID) and a Sri Lankan bank account. Some lenders may request a phone number or proof of income.

Is it safe to apply through FinSriLanka?

Absolutely. We only work with trusted, verified lenders using secure digital platforms. Your personal data is protected with industry-standard encryption.

What happens after I click "Apply"?

You’ll be redirected to the lender’s secure website to complete your loan application in just a few minutes.

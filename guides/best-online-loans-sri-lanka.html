How we compare “best” online loans

This shortlist focuses on online personal loans in Sri Lanka with transparent pricing, clear eligibility and quick decisions. We look at published limits and term ranges, typical approval time after e-KYC, clarity of fees, and whether first-time customers can access promotional 0% offers.

Typical amounts & terms

New users often receive smaller limits that can grow with positive repayment history. Common ranges are a few thousand rupees up to around LKR 200,000 for repeat borrowers. Terms are usually short-term — for example, 65–180 days — with scheduled repayments.

Typical decision time

With digital KYC and bank verification, decisions may arrive in minutes. Manual review can take longer, especially if submitted data is inconsistent or there are issues with bank matching. Keep your phone reachable to avoid delays.

Basic eligibility

  • Age commonly 18–60 (provider-specific).
  • Valid NIC and a Sri Lankan bank account in your name.
  • Working phone number for OTP and verification calls.

Intro 0% promotions

Some providers run 0% intro for first-time borrowers. Limits, availability and conditions vary by lender; caps may apply. These promotions are useful to build repayment history if you repay on time.

How to choose & apply

  1. Check Top Offers for the amount/term you need and the typical decision time.
  2. Prepare NIC photos and bank details in advance; submit consistent data.
  3. Verify identity and bank, review total cost and due dates, then accept.

FAQ

Are online loans legal in Sri Lanka? Yes — apply only with licensed providers and via official websites/apps.

Will early repayment reduce cost? Often yes for short-term products. Check the lender’s terms.

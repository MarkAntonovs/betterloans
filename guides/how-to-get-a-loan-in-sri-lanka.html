How to Get a Personal Loan in Sri Lanka (2025)

Eligibility, required documents, typical approval times, and friendly tips to increase your chances. Last updated: Aug 30, 2025.

1) Check your eligibility

  • Age: typically 18–60 (varies by lender).
  • Valid Sri Lankan NIC (National Identity Card).
  • Active Sri Lankan bank account.
  • Reachable phone number and stable internet access.

2) Prepare documents

  • NIC (front & back photos).
  • Bank account details for disbursement.
  • Sometimes proof of income (depends on lender).

3) Apply online

Choose a licensed provider, fill the short form, verify your identity, and submit. Decisions are often fast.

4) Tips to increase approval chances

  • Use accurate, up-to-date personal data — mismatches cause rejections.
  • Keep your phone reachable for verification calls.
  • Start with smaller limits and build a positive repayment history.

Quick summary

  • Decision: ~10–60 minutes
  • Basics: NIC + active bank account
  • Intro 0%: may be available

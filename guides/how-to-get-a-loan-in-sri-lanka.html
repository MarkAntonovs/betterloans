How to Get a Personal Loan in Sri Lanka (2025)

Eligibility, documents, application steps, and friendly tips to increase your chances.

On this page
Check your eligibility Prepare documents Apply online Approval tips FAQ

Typical decision time: ~10–60 minutes. You’ll need a valid NIC and an active bank account.

1) Check your eligibility

  • Age: typically 18–60 (varies by lender).
  • Sri Lankan NIC (National Identity Card).
  • Active Sri Lankan bank account.
  • Reachable phone number and stable internet access.

2) Prepare documents

  • NIC (front & back photos).
  • Bank details for disbursement.
  • Sometimes proof of income (varies by lender).

3) Apply online

Choose a licensed provider, fill the short form, verify your identity, and submit. Decisions are often fast.

4) Tips to increase approval chances

  • Use accurate, up-to-date personal data — mismatches cause rejections.
  • Keep your phone reachable for verification calls.
  • Start with smaller limits and build a positive repayment history.

FAQ

Do I get 0% on the first loan? Some providers offer introductory 0% interest to first-time borrowers (availability varies).

How fast is approval? Many users get a decision within minutes; exact time depends on the lender.

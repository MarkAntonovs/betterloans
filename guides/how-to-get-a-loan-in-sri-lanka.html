How to Get a Personal Loan in Sri Lanka (2025)

Eligibility, required documents, typical decision time and practical tips to increase approval chances.

On this page
1) Check your eligibility 2) Prepare your documents 3) Apply online 4) Tips to increase approval chances FAQ

We are not a lender. Always apply with licensed providers and review their fees and T&Cs.

1) Check your eligibility

  • Age: usually 18–60 (varies by lender).
  • Valid Sri Lankan NIC (National Identity Card).
  • Active bank account in your name for disbursement.
  • Reachable phone number and internet access.

2) Prepare your documents

  • NIC (front & back photos).
  • Bank account details for disbursement.
  • Sometimes proof of income (depends on lender).

3) Apply online

Choose a licensed provider, fill the short form, verify your identity and submit. Decisions are often fast — many same-day.

4) Tips to increase approval chances

  • Use accurate, up-to-date personal data — mismatches cause rejections.
  • Keep your phone reachable for verification calls.
  • Start with smaller limits and build a positive repayment history.

FAQ

Can I get 0% on the first loan? Some providers offer intro 0% to first-time borrowers who repay on time; conditions vary.

How fast is approval? Typically ~10–60 minutes depending on KYC and load.
